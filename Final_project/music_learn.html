Close your eyes, when all senses are reduced to only hearing, what remains in your world...

<Ear> is the fifth solo album by singer Li Ronghao, released on October 17, 2018. It is the most successful album in his career so far, both commercially and in terms of personal style. The retro concept and arrangement style of this album have led the Chinese Mandopop music scene into a new era.

The album includes ten singles, which are:

  1. Quarrelsome Lovers
  2. Dwell On The Past
  3. Poverty Or Wealth
  4. The Band
  5. I Know It's You
  6. Ear
  7. If I Were Young
  8. The Weight Of Life
  9. Zhang Jia Ming And Wan Jun
  10. Babe

Due to my obsession and admiration for this album, I meticulously transcribed and arranged the album's title track by ear. This is a tribute to that retro golden era, and also to Li Ronghao's outstanding contributions to the Chinese music scene. The video on the left is ear(music composition), the left one is piano singing(another song: adorations).
